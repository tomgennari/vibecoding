## 2. Description of Service

The Platform is an educational entertainment and fundraising service designed to finance the construction of the unified Campus of St. Andrew's Scots School (SASS) in San Fernando, Province of Buenos Aires, Argentina.

The Platform allows users to:
- Access and play video games created by members of the SASS community
- Publish self-developed video games (students only)
- Make payments to unlock additional content
- Make voluntary donations for campus construction (parents and family members)
- Participate in competitions between the school's Houses

All funds raised through the Platform are directed entirely to the Campus San Andrés construction fund, with only the MercadoPago payment processor commission deducted.
