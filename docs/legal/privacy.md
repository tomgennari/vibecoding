## 1. Introduction

vibecoding.ar, operator of the Campus San Andrés / Vibe Coding San Andrés platform (hereinafter "the Platform"), is committed to protecting the privacy of all its users, with special attention to the protection of minors' data.

This Privacy Policy describes what data we collect, how we use it, how we protect it, and what your rights are under **Argentine Law 25,326 on Personal Data Protection** and its regulatory standards.

---

## 2. Data We Collect

### 2.1 Data provided by the user upon registration
- First and last name
- Email address
- Password (stored in encrypted format — never in plain text)
- User type (student or parent/family member)
- Chosen House (William Brown, James Dodds, James Fleming, or John Monteith)

### 2.2 Data generated by Platform use
- Game history and time spent on each game
- Scores obtained in games
- Games unlocked through payment
- Likes given to games
- Games submitted for publication (students only)
- Date and time of Platform access

### 2.3 Payment and donation data
- Amount and date of each transaction
- MercadoPago transaction identifier
- History of unlocks and donations

**Important:** vibecoding.ar does **not store** credit card data, banking data, or sensitive financial information. This data is processed and stored exclusively by MercadoPago under their own security policies.

### 2.4 Technical data
- IP address (for security and fraud prevention)
- Device type and browser
- Technical activity logs

### 2.5 What we do NOT collect
- Profile photos or user images
- Geographic location
- Biometric data
- Health condition information
- Social media data

---

## 3. Purpose of Data Processing

Collected data is used exclusively for:

1. **Account management:** creating, maintaining, and administering user accounts
2. **Service delivery:** enabling access to games and Platform features
3. **Payment processing:** managing transactions through MercadoPago
4. **Gamification:** calculating points, rankings, and House scoreboards
5. **Moderation:** reviewing content submitted by students to ensure safety
6. **Communications:** sending transactional emails (registration confirmation, game approval/rejection, payment confirmation)
7. **Security:** detecting and preventing fraud, unauthorized access, and misuse
8. **Service improvement:** aggregated and anonymous analysis of Platform usage

**Personal data is not used for advertising purposes and is not shared with third parties for marketing.**

---

## 4. Protection of Minors' Data

The Platform is directed at St. Andrew's Scots School students, who are mostly minors. We apply special protection measures:

- We collect the **minimum personal data** necessary for the service to function
- We do not request or store profile photos or images of minors
- There is no chat, messaging, or direct communication between users
- Games submitted by students undergo manual review before publication
- Registration of minors implies consent from a parent or legal guardian

**If you are a parent or guardian of a registered minor and wish to review, modify, or delete their data, contact us at vibes@vibecoding.ar.**

## 5. Sharing Data with Third Parties

vibecoding.ar shares data with third parties only in the following cases:

| Third Party | Data Shared | Reason |
|-------------|-------------|--------|
| **Supabase** (USA) | All user data | Database and authentication provider |
| **Vercel** (USA) | Technical access data | Hosting provider |
| **MercadoPago** (Argentina) | Transaction data | Payment processing |

These providers act as data processors and are contractually obligated to protect your data in accordance with international security standards.

**vibecoding.ar does not sell, rent, or commercialize users' personal data under any circumstances.**

---

## 6. Data Security

We implement the following technical measures to protect your data:

- **Encryption:** passwords are stored with bcrypt hashing. Communications are conducted over HTTPS/TLS
- **Restricted access:** Row Level Security (RLS) in the database — each user only accesses their own data
- **Secure authentication:** JWT tokens with automatic expiration
- **Minimization:** we only collect strictly necessary data
- **No payment data:** we do not store card data or sensitive financial information

## 8. Your Rights (Law 25,326)

Under Argentine Law 25,326 on Personal Data Protection, you have the right to:

- **Access:** request information about what data we store about you
- **Rectification:** correct inaccurate or outdated data
- **Deletion:** request the erasure of your data ("right to be forgotten")
- **Confidentiality:** your data not be transferred to unauthorized third parties
- **Opposition:** object to the processing of your data for certain purposes

To exercise any of these rights, send an email to **vibes@vibecoding.ar** with the subject "Privacy Rights" and clearly indicate which right you wish to exercise. We will respond within 5 business days.

The DNPDP (National Directorate for Personal Data Protection) is the competent supervisory authority: **www.argentina.gob.ar/aaip/datospersonales**
